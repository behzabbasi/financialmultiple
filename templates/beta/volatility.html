Cboe Volatility Index (VIX) & Cboe NASDAQ-100 Volatility Index (VXN):

The VIX Index is based on real-time prices of options on the S&P 500 Index (SPX) and is designed to reflect investors' consensus view of future (30-day) expected stock market volatility. The VIX Index is often referred to as the market's "fear gauge". The Cboe NASDAQ-100 Volatility Index (VXN) is a key measure of market expectations of near-term volatility conveyed by NASDAQ-100 Index (NDX) option prices. It measures the market's expectation of 30-day volatility implicit in the prices of near-term NASDAQ-100 options.

Source: Cboe Exchange, Inc.
